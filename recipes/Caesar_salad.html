Caesar salad

Description

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Fresh minced garlic
  • Chicken
  • Roasted toast
  • Salt and pepper
  • Tomato's

How to make Caesar salad

  1. Cut chicken and put on the pan
  2. Put toast in toaster
  3. Cut tomato's and garlic
  4. When chicken and toast ready, cut them small pieces
  5. Now take bowl or plate, to put thing's in
  6. When everything is in bowl/plate than put on mayo, salt and pepper and then mix
