Rice with chicken

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Lemon

How to make Rice with chicken

  1. Cut chicken to small pieces and season them
  2. Cook the chicken on pan
  3. Boil a medium pot of water for rice
  4. While chicken is cooking, take out the rice
  5. When both things are ready, you can squeeze lemon on rice and chicken
