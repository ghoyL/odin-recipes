French toast

Description

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Toast
  • In addition: banana's, blueberries etc

How to make a French toast

  1. Mix 3 eggs, milk and sugar in bowl
  2. Soaking bread, and then putting on the pan
  3. Wait until both sides are golden
  4. When toast is ready slide it onto a plate
  5. That's it
